    Bio

    Born in Oklahoma, USA. Stephen graduated in marine biology. In 1987, he started to learn animation and besides the SpongeBob, he worked in amount others animations on Nickelodeon, like Rugrats. Sthepen died of ALS (Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis), a desease of the nervous system that weakness the muscles and imparis physical functions. Hillenburg worked from cartoonist, animator, marine biologist and voice actor. We don't have a lot of informations of your life, but the basic of we have shows that this man was be an impressive and creative creator, making a childhood of so many people in the world, either with his jokes or with his teachings of humility and friendship.
